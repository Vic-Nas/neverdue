# [NeverDue](https://neverdue.ca)

**Never miss a deadline again.**

NeverDue watches your inbox and automatically adds deadlines, appointments, and events to your Google Calendar — so nothing slips through the cracks.

---

## How it works

1. Sign in with Google
2. Forward emails to your personal NeverDue address (e.g. `yourname@user.neverdue.ca`)
3. NeverDue extracts the dates and adds them straight to your calendar

That's it. No manual entry, no copy-pasting dates.

## Privacy

Your emails are processed and immediately discarded. We never store raw email content. You can revoke Google Calendar access at any time.
